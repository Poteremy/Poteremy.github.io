How To Play

To add new inputs, press the "Add Input" button

In the new word input, insert the type of word. (i.e. "noun", "adjective", etc.)

Type your story into the box under the story title input.

To indicate a place where a user input word will appear in the story, simple type the number corresponding with that input.

When completed, press the "Play It" button to have someone play your Madlib.
